Welcome to Ibiza Life!

Whether it is golden beaches with beautiful blue waters, clubs the size of cathedrals and some of the best sunsets in the world, Ibiza has it all.

Together with gorgeous coastlines, boat trips and hiking opportunities, you have the recipe for one of the most appealing and diverse islands in the world.

An island in the Mediterranean Sea off the eastern coast of Spain, Ibiza is the third largest of the Balearic Islands.

Ibiza is well known for its association with nightlife, electronic dance music and for the summer club scene, all of which attract large numbers of tourists.

Although it has some of the best nightclubs in the world, Ibiza is diverse, and there are plenty of things to enjoy whilst on the island.

Cala Bassa beach on the West coast of Ibiza offers crystal clear blue waters with lovely white sand, whilst there are pine trees that run along the back of the beach offering a nice shade from the blazing sun. Accessible via a 15-30 minute ferry ride from the San Antonio front near the Egg.

The small and quiet resort of Portinatx in the North of the island offers three beautiful beaches - S'Arenal Gros; little S'Arenal Petit and Playa Porto Beach, all with amazing clear waters and white sand.

On the East of the island is the beautiful beach of Cala Llenya, which is surrounded by pine trees and excellent turquoise waters. It's a popular beach with families and holiday makers but is never too busy, meaning you'll get an area to yourself even during the peak summers.
